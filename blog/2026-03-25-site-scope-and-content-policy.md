This website separates content into three tracks: Notes, Blog, and Projects.

<!-- truncate -->

- Notes are quick personal understanding and can contain mistakes.
- Blog posts are formal publications with stronger validation.
- Projects pages document implementation details and architecture decisions.

This separation keeps daily learning fast while preserving a reliable place for finalized ideas.
